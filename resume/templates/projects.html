Senior Year Thesis As part of my senior thesis at Reed College, I designed a neural network that performs the task of sentiment analysis on Hinglish (code-mixed Hindi-English) tweets. Web-Caching System For a project in my Systems class, my partner and I implemented a web-cache with a RESTful API that allowed multiple clients over the multithreaded server to access the cache. The design of our cache was based on Facebook's Memcache, and required a lot of test driven design strategies. This was my first introduction to Boost.Beast, a C++ module that simplifies building client-server applications.

Branch.io Chat Platform I am a big believer in accessibility on the internet. The idea is to build a chat application that allows users to branch their conversation and keep track of it as their train of thought diverges. This was aimed to serve everybody's natural tendencies to branch conversations, and also ended up being useful for users with ADHD to keep track of their thoughts. This was my first attempt at helping create a more accessible internet and made me appreciate the vast differences in people's experiences while using technology.
